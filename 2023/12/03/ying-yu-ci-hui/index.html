喷漆英语句子

清洁/喷漆部分应提供清洁和喷漆一个或多个区域所需的信息，并包含保护人员和材料所需的安全预防措施

The cleaning/painting portion shall provide information necessary to clean and paint an area or areas,and contain safety precautions necessary to protect personnel and material.

1、Mask off the adjacent area, which will not be abraded, with masking tape and paper. Refer to the following instructions and SRM 51-75-11.

使用胶带和纸保护不需要打磨的区域。参考下述说明及SRM 51-75-11。

2、Main and nose landing gears. Perform with care the masking of all landing gear and wheel well, in order to avoid paint vaporization on internal surface of landing gear doors and wheel wells.

主起落架和前起落架。起落架和轮舱要仔细地保护好，避免油漆气雾进入内表面。

3、In accordance with SRM 51-75-11, perform the sanding process on composite materials and metallic surfaces. Lightly abrade the surface with emery cloth (start with grade 180 or finer and finish with grade 320) until the layer of primer is exposed.

根据飞机维修手册SRM 51-75-11，执行在复合材料和金属表面的打磨程序。用砂纸(开始用180目或更细，最后用320目)轻轻打磨，直到底漆层暴露。

4、CAUTION: Never use chemical stripper on composite. Some composite panels can metalized (aluminum mesh, aluminum foil).Take care of not damaging metallization during the sanding process. During sanding process, take care of not damaging composite fiber.

注意：绝对不可以用化学物品褪漆剂在复合材料上。有些面板里面会有金属网或金属铝箔，打磨过程中要小心不用损伤它们。打磨过程中，要小心不要磨伤玻璃纤维。

5、Renewal of paint build-up of composite components per SRM 51-75-12 and SRM 51-23-11.

参考SRM 51-75-12和SRM 51-23-11恢复复合材料零件表面漆层组成

6、Before painting, degrease all surfaces to be painted using cotton wipers dampened with cleaner(refer to SRM 51-75-12), and dry with a cloth.

喷漆之前，要喷漆的表面用浸泡过清洁剂(参考SRM 51-75-12)的棉布去除油脂，并用一块干布擦干。

7、Make sure of the exact position of premask, and inspect masking before paint.

确保模板的准确位置，并检查喷漆之前的保护。

8、Make sure the applied topcoat films with good quality, record in APPENDIX 5 “TOP COAT APPLICATION RECORD”.

确保所喷的漆膜质量是好的，并在附件5《面漆施工记录表》内做记录。

9、Restensil and technical marking should be installed according to AMM CH 11 and customer decoration drawing.

重新做回标识要依照AMM 11章节和客户装饰图纸(Drawing NO./图纸号：

10.After completion of the different tasks, make sure that the masking materials are well removed.

完成所有不同工作后，确保所有保护材料全部清除。
