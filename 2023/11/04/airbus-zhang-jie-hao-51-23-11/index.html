51-23-11 - Description - Paint Coatings

51-23-11 -描述-油漆涂料

CAUTION: DO NOT USE METAL TOOLS TO REMOVE THE UNWANTED MATERIAL. METAL TOOLS CAN CAUSE DAMAGE TO THE SURFACE OF THE COMPONENT.

1.General

A.This section gives you information about the paint scheme of the aircraft. Refer to supplier information for application and properties of each paint coating. For special coatings refer to Chapter 51-23-12.

B.Each product, which is given in the paint scheme tables by the material numbers in Paragraph 2..A., can be determined in CML.

C.If you have to repair the original surface protection, for example after corrosion removal or structural repair, refer to Chapter 51-75-12.

2.Procedure

A.Paint Scheme

(1)The subsequent parts are not painted:

-

leading edge of slats (bonding primer only) and leading edge of engine inlet cowl,

-

external surface of the pylon made of corrosion resistant materials (stainless steel/titanium),

-

passenger and bulk cargo door scuff plates,

-

APU exhaust,

-

equipment components, for example angle of attack sensors and static ports,

-

removed or internal side of cabin pressurization Control System Outflow Valve (Zone 172),

-

refer to Chapter 51-75-12 for repainting of the external face of the avionics skin temperature sensor (28HQ) support,

-

ball lock fittings.

B.

Paint Coatings

(1)

Pretreatment

For information about chromic acid anodizing, wash primer (Material No. 04CMA2), surface pretreatment (Material No. 10ABC1) and epoxy primer (Material No. 16-006B or Material No. 16-006C), refer to Chapter 51-21-11.

(2)

Primer

(a)

General

1

The primer is composed of a polyurethane or epoxy basis, an activator (catalyst) or hardener and, if required, a thinner. The different primers which are used for the aircraft protection can be divided into two main groups:

-

primer with chromate.

-

primer without chromate.

(3)

Top Coat and Finish Paint

(a)

General

1

Top coat and finish paint are composed of a polyurethane basis, a hardener and, if required, a thinner. The two coatings used for the aircraft protection are:

-

top coat polyurethane (Material No. 04JME4) for internal areas.

-

top coat polyurethane (Material No. 04JAA3) for external areas.
