2.Safety Precautions

NOTE: When you do repainting near AOA sensors, we recommend that you mask the sensors.

WARNING: BE CAREFUL WHEN YOU USE CONSUMABLE MATERIALS. OBEY THE MATERIAL MANUFACTURER’S INSTRUCTIONS AND YOUR LOCAL REGULATIONS.

WARNING: OBEY THE MATERIAL MANUFACTURER INSTRUCTIONS WHEN YOU USE PAINTS, CHROMATE AGENTS, CLEANING AGENTS AND SPECIAL COATINGS. MOST OF THESE MATERIALS ARE DANGEROUS AND FLAMMABLE.

WARNING: DO NOT APPLY ADHESIVE TAPE ON THE PROBES, DUCTS AND SENSORS (STATIC PITOT, TAT, AOA). USE ONLY THE SPECIFIED TOOLS FOR THE PROTECTION OF THE AIRCRAFT.

THE SPECIFIED TOOLS GIVE THE CORRECT PROTECTION TO THE AIRCRAFT EQUIPMENT. THE SPECIFIED TOOLS ARE EASY TO SEE FROM THE GROUND. THE SPECIFIED TOOLS ARE EASY TO REMOVE.

IF YOU USE TAPE, THERE IS A RISK THAT SOME TAPE, OR ADHESIVE FROM THE TAPE, WILL STAY ON THE PROBES, DUCTS OR SENSORS. THIS CAN CAUSE INCORRECT INDICATIONS ON THE RELATED COCKPIT INSTRUMENTS.

WARNING: MAKE SURE THAT THE AOA HEATER OR MFP CIRCUIT BREAKERS ARE PULLED DURING STRIPPING, PAINTING AND HANDLING OF AOA. LET THE AOA/MFP COOL DOWN A FEW MINUTES BEFORE MASKING OR HANDLING
