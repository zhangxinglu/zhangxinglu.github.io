B.Paint thickness requirements

B .油漆厚度要求

(1) Except for radome, antennas and areas covered with anti erosion coating, the maximum recommended paint thickness is 350 microns (13780 microinches) and 400 microns (15748 microinches) for nacelles. If the recommended paint thickness is exceeded, this can cause electrical discharge which can interfere with the communication and navigation systems.
(1)除雷达罩、天线和覆盖防侵蚀涂层的区域外，建议最大涂层厚度为350微米(13780微英寸)，机舱最大涂层厚度为400微米(15748微英寸)。如果超过推荐的涂料厚度，这可能会导致放电，从而干扰通信和导航系统。

2.Safety Precautions

2.安全预防措施

NOTE: When you do repainting near AOA sensors, we recommend that you mask the sensors.
提示: 当您在 AOA 传感器附近重新喷漆时，我们建议您遮蔽传感器。

WARNING: BE CAREFUL WHEN YOU USE CONSUMABLE MATERIALS. OBEY THE MATERIAL MANUFACTURER’S INSTRUCTIONS AND YOUR LOCAL REGULATIONS.
警告: 使用消耗材料时要小心。遵守材料制造商的指示和当地的规章制度。

WARNING: OBEY THE MATERIAL MANUFACTURER INSTRUCTIONS WHEN YOU USE PAINTS, CHROMATE AGENTS, CLEANING AGENTS AND SPECIAL COATINGS. MOST OF THESE MATERIALS ARE DANGEROUS AND FLAMMABLE.
警告: 使用油漆、铬酸盐剂、清洁剂和特殊涂料时，请遵守材料制造商的指示。这些材料大部分都是危险易燃的。

WARNING: DO NOT APPLY ADHESIVE TAPE ON THE PROBES, DUCTS AND SENSORS (STATIC PITOT, TAT, AOA). USE ONLY THE SPECIFIED TOOLS FOR THE PROTECTION OF THE AIRCRAFT.
警告: 不要在探头、导管和传感器上使用胶带(静态皮托管、 TAT、 AOA)。只使用指定的工具保护飞机。

THE SPECIFIED TOOLS GIVE THE CORRECT PROTECTION TO THE AIRCRAFT EQUIPMENT. THE SPECIFIED TOOLS ARE EASY TO SEE FROM THE GROUND. THE SPECIFIED TOOLS ARE EASY TO REMOVE.
指定的工具对飞机设备提供正确的保护。指定的工具在地面上很容易看到。指定的工具很容易移除。

IF YOU USE TAPE, THERE IS A RISK THAT SOME TAPE, OR ADHESIVE FROM THE TAPE, WILL STAY ON THE PROBES, DUCTS OR SENSORS. THIS CAN CAUSE INCORRECT INDICATIONS ON THE RELATED COCKPIT INSTRUMENTS.
如果你使用胶带，有些胶带，或者胶带上的粘合剂，会留在探头，管道或者传感器上。这会导致相关驾驶舱仪表指示错误。

WARNING: MAKE SURE THAT THE AOA HEATER OR MFP CIRCUIT BREAKERS ARE PULLED DURING STRIPPING, PAINTING AND HANDLING OF AOA. LET THE AOA/MFP COOL DOWN A FEW MINUTES BEFORE MASKING OR HANDLING
警告: 确保 AOA 加热器或 MFP 断路器在脱漆、喷漆和处理 AOA 过程中被拉动。在遮蔽或处理前让 AOA/MFP 冷却几分钟

CAUTION: IF THE STANDARD CERTIFIED COLOR BAC 707 IS NOT TO BE USED TO RESTORE PAINT OR REPAINTING OF THE UPPER SURFACES OFTHE HORIZONTAL TAILPLANE, ELEVATOR, AILERONS, SPOILERS AND FLAPS, PLEASE CONTACT AIRBUS FOR APPROVAL OF THE NON-STANDARD PAINT COLOR.
注意: 如果水平尾翼、升降舵、副翼、扰流板和襟翼的上表面不能用 BAC 707认证的标准颜色修复或重新油漆，请与空中客车公司联系确认非标准颜色。

CAUTION: MAKE SURE THAT THE MATERIALS, AREAS AND PARTS LISTED ARE PROPERLY PROTECTED AGAINST PAINT MATERIALS BY MASKING (RUBBER, ALL COMPOSITE PARTS, ACRYLIC MATERIALS, AERODYNAMIC SMOOTHER, METAL BONDED EDGES, ANTENNAS, PITOT PROBES, SENSORS, STATIC PORTS, ENGINE AIR INTAKE, PRECOOLER AIR OUTLET SCREEN, ENGINE EXHAUST DUCT, APU EXHAUST, APU INTAKES AND OUTLETS, AIR CONDITIONING FRESH AIR INTAKE, CABIN PRESSURIZATION CONTROL SYSTEM OUTFLOW VALVE (ZONE 172), LANDING GEARS, DOOR SEALS, ACCESS DOORS, CABIN WINDOW AND WINDSHIELD PANELS AND SEALS, ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT AND CABLES, PLASTIC MATERIALS, EXTERNAL SKIN PANEL JOINTS, HIGH STRENGTH STEEL PARTS, AVIONICS SKIN TEMPERATURE SENSOR (28HQ) SUPPORT EXTERNAL FACE) OR BY PLUGGING (DRAIN HOLES, VENTS).
注意: 确保列出的材料、区域和部件有适当的防油漆材料的遮蔽保护(橡胶、所有复合材料零件、丙烯酸材料、空气动力缓冲器、金属粘接边、天线、皮托管探头、传感器、静止端口、发动机进气口、预冷器出气屏幕、发动机排气管道、空气净化器排气口、空气净化器进气口和出气口、空气调节进气口、机舱加压控制系统出气阀(172区)、起落架、门封、出入口门、客舱窗和窗玻璃板和密封件、电气设备和 C采购产品 ABLES，塑料材料，外部皮肤面板接头，高强度钢部件，航空皮肤温度传感器(28总部)支持外部面)或通过堵塞(排水孔，通风口)。

C. Repair

C.维修

(1) Remove any loose paint with a plastic scraper.
（1)用塑料刮刀刮掉任何松动的油漆。

(2) Use an adhesive tape to check the adhesion of the remaining paint.

(2)用胶带检查剩余油漆的附着力。

(3) Mask off the adjacent area, which will not be treated, with masking tape and paper.

(3)用胶带和纸张遮盖邻近不会被处理的区域。

(4) Abrade the surface with emery cloth (320 grade) and chamfer the edges of the remaining paint around the damaged area.

(4)用砂布(320级)对表面进行打磨，将破损区域周围残留的油漆边缘倒角。

(5) Remove the abrasive grit, clean and degrease the surface with cleaning agent (Material No. 08BBF1 or 08BAA9).
(5)除去磨料，用清洁剂(物料编号08BBF1或08BAA9)清洁及除油。

NOTE: Alternative solvent according to AIMS 09-00-002 can be used.
提示: 可使用 AIMS 09-00-002规定的替代溶剂。

(6) Apply epoxy primer (Material No. 04DAB2 or 04DBA3) or polyurethane primer (Material No. 04EAC2) or for external surface of fuselage/VTP/nacelle low voc chromate free epoxy primer (Material No. 04DCA3) to the abraded area.
(6)在磨损部位涂上环氧底漆(材料编号04DAB2或04DABA3)或聚氨酯底漆(材料编号04EAC2)或机身/VTP/吊舱无低挥发性有机化合物环氧底漆(材料编号04DCA3)的外表面。

NOTE: Make sure that the ambient temperature and the skin temperature is above 20 °C (68.0 °F) during mixing, application and curing of Material No. 04DCA3.

注意: 04DCA3材料在混合、使用和固化过程中，环境温度和表皮温度必须在20 °C (68.0 ° F)以上。

(7) Apply the appropriate polyurethane top coat (Refer to Chapter 51-23-11).

C.修复

(1)Remove top coat and primer in accordance with Chapter 51-75-11.

(1)按照第51-75-11章的规定拆卸面漆和底漆。

(2)Lightly abrade with SCOTCH-BRITE or abrasive paper grade 240 or finer.

(2)用SCOTCH-BRITE 或砂纸等级240或以上轻微研磨。

(3)Clean and degrease the surface with cleaning agent (Material No. 08BBF1 or 08BBD1 or 08BAA9).
(3)用清洁剂(物料编号08BBF1或08BBD1或08BAA9)清洁及除去表面的油脂。

NOTE: Alternative solvent can be used, refer to AIMS 09-00-002.

注: 可使用替代溶剂，请参阅 AIMS 09-00-002。

(4)屏蔽掉不需要重新粉刷的区域，参见第4段。

(5)用清洁剂(物料编号08BBF1或08BBD1或11-0206)清除表面的污垢
