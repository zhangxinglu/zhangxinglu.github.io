NOTE: These instructions are for the finishes of a radome without a boot.

NOTE: Thicknesses of paint which are more than 0.01 in. (0.254 mm) can decrease the necessary

radome electrical transmission efficiency. A radome wall that has been repainted should be

put back to its initial thickness, including paint thickness. If this is not done, radome

transmission efficiency will be decreased (SRM 53-10-72-2R-0).

SUBTASK 53-52-00-370-004

the next layer:

\3) Solvent clean using alcohol, B50073.

\4) If necessary, use tack rag, G50123, to remove particulate contamination.

\5) Visually examine the anti-static coating surface to make sure that the coating is

continuous (no bare areas).
