2. BMS10-72 Topcoat Application

A. General

(1)

This task has steps to do the following.

(a) Prepare to apply BMS10-72 topcoat

(b) Apply BMS10-72 topcoat

(2) Boeing recommends you apply the BMS10-72 topcoat when the ambient air temperature is

between 65°F (18°C) and 95°F (35°C).

(a) Contact the paint manufacturer for guidance if you will apply the paint outside the

recommended temperature range.

(3) Boeing recommends you apply the BMS10-72 topcoat when the relative humidity is less than

70%.

(a) Contact the paint manufacturer for guidance if you will apply the paint when the relative

humidity is greater than 70%.

E. Prepare to Apply the BMS10-72 Topcoat

SUBTASK 51-21-73-950-006

(1) Mask the surface adjacent to the area you will apply topcoat.

(a) Attach the tags that follow to the left control wheel in the flight deck.

\1) “PITOT PROBES COVERED” tag, G02447

\2) “STATIC PORTS COVERED” tag, G02444

\3) A red tag labeled “AOA SENSORS COVERED”

(b) Put a pitot probe cover, COM-1503 on the pitot probe.

(c) Apply Scotch Flatback Masking Tape 250, G00270 on the static ports.

(d) Do the steps that follow to cover the angle-of-attack (AOA) sensors.

\1) Cover the angle-of-attack sensors with PG-777 solvent-resistant masking tape.

a) Apply PG-777 over the blade, plate, and blade-to-plate gap.

NOTE: This is to allow easier removal of 3M 425 aluminum tape.

\2) Cover the PG-777 solvent-resistant masking tape with 3M 425 Aluminum Foil Tape,

G00291.

a)

Shingle the aluminum foil tape from the blade-to-plate gap up to the blade tip

until all of the masking tape is covered.
